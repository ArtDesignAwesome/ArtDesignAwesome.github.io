Just Vibin' Privacy Policy

Just Vibin' creates local AI voice renders on-device after downloading the selected local model files. Voice samples and generated audio are intended to remain in app-controlled local storage unless you choose to export or share them.

The app is free and ad-supported. Google AdMob may process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and ad interaction data according to Google's advertising and privacy policies. A one-time Remove Ads purchase is available.

Only create or clone voices you own, have permission to use, or are otherwise allowed to use.
